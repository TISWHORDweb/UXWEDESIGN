HR recruitment app

Hirved is a startup focusing on the recruitment technology solutions for companies. The founder noticed that recruiters struggle with recruiting or hiring the right talents. They saw an opportunity to create a recruitment app that would help find and hire talent more efficiently with the help of Artificial Intelligence. The goal is to develop a recruitment app that allows recruiters to post job openings, search for candidates and manage the hiring process all in one app. Other features will include communication with candidates and recruiters, schedule interviews and make job offers.

Outcome

Increased Attraction from Business Incubators / VCs.

16 weeks

Shuttlers - charter a bus

Shuttlers charter service allows vehicle owners add their vehicle on shuttlers and earn money taking trips on shuttlers and allows customers looking to charter a vehicle find and charter a vehicle stress free. The goal of the project is to design an engaging and functioning web app for users, and dashboard for back-end use People looking to charter a vehicle can find vehicle that suits their needs and charter it easily. When customers request for vehicles on our service, we route the request to vehicle owner and earn a markup of 10 - 30% on each charter request.

Outcome

10X Improved Engagement, Positive User Experience

12 weeks

Fitfactory - CRM Redesign

The main objective of the redesign was to improve the user experience, simplify the interface, and increase adoption of the app by making it more intuitive and user-friendly. We conducted user research, including interviews and surveys, to understand the pain points and needs of the app's target users. We also analyzed usage data to identify areas of improvement. Based on our research, we created a new information architecture and user flow to simplify the navigation and make it more intuitive. We also focused on creating a clear visual hierarchy to guide the user's eye to the most important information and calls to action.

Outcome

User engagement and adoption increased by 20%, Reduction in support requests

12 weeks
